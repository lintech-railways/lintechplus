Subject: Naromoru Mission 17th - 30th Nov 2019

From Newlife Church Secretary, Nov 9, 2019.

Reply Forward

It is yet another time of the year that the SDA Church Newlife at 5th Ngong Ave -Nairobi is inviting all of us to partner with Jesus in reaching out to the World. In the years past, the church has shared the love of God with brethren in places like Kyevaluki, Miteitei, Awasi,Tharaka Nithi, Kangundo, Tala and Matuu where many were led to giving their lives to the Lordship of Jesus Christ. From 17th-30th November 2019 the entire SDA Church- Newlife family will be setting camp in Naromoru near the slopes of Mt. Kenya.

During this time, we hope to share the love and truth about our Lord Jesus with the children of God in this region as we minister to them both physically and spiritually. The Naromoru evangelism organizing team invites you to support this mission through your prayers, offerings and presence.

Thank you again for your registration. If you have any questions, please let me know!
